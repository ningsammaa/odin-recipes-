Chocolate Chips Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are a popular type of baked treat made from a simple cookie dough that is studded with chocolate chips or chunks. They are one of the most beloved and recognizable types of cookies around the world. The cookies are typically characterized by their soft and chewy texture, with a slightly crispy exterior.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips or chunks
  • Optional: Chopped nuts (such as walnuts or pecans)

    • Keep in mind that specific recipes may vary, and some variations might include additional ingredients or substitutions.

The cooking process:

  1. Pre-heat your oven to the temperature specified in the recipe (usually around 350°F or 175°C).
  2. Measure out all the ingredients and have them ready for use.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, ensuring each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next. Mix in the vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  7. Gently fold in the chocolate chips (or chunks) and chopped nuts, if using.
  8. Use a cookie scoop or spoon to drop rounded tablespoons of cookie dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Leave enough space between each cookie for them to spread while baking.
  9. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for the time specified in the recipe, usually around 8-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are set.
  10. Once baked, remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool on the sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Once the cookies have cooled, they are ready to be enjoyed! Serve them with a glass of milk or your favorite beverage.
